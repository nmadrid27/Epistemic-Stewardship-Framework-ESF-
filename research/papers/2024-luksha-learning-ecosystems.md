## Key Findings

- Educational institutions need methodologies capable of evolving with technological and social change; static frameworks become obsolete without built-in revision mechanisms.
- Argues for adaptive learning systems that build in structural mechanisms for self-revision, not just best-practice snapshots.
- Learning ecosystems must be designed for emergence and transformation, not optimization for current conditions.
