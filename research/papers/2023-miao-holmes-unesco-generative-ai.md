## Key Findings

- Establishes transparency as a policy requirement for AI use in educational contexts: AI involvement should be clearly documented and disclosed.
- Addresses governance and boundaries of AI use in education without structuring the practice of AI-assisted work itself.
- Advocates for institutional policies that balance enabling AI's benefits with protecting academic integrity and student development.
